\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{classdi_1_1uniba_1_1map_1_1b_1_1adventure_1_1parser_1_1_parser}{Parser} class is responsible for interpreting player commands within the game. It processes input strings, removing stopwords, and identifies commands and objects mentioned in the input. It supports simple sentences structured as $<$action$>$ $<$object$>$ $<$object$>$, with articles and prepositions being ignored.
